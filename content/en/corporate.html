Hub of Americas

Copa Airlines' Hub of the Americas, one of the most successful connection centers in Latin America, located in Tocumen International Airport, offers the most direct way to connect to the Americas due to: Panama’s geographic location and favorable climate conditions allows the airport to operate 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Fast connections without the need to clear immigration and customs. Two state of the art runways to ensure traffic is moving smoothly in and out.

Alliances

Copa Airlines and its strategic partners work together to offer the highest quality service, convenience, and completion for your travel experience. Our code-share alliances allow us to offer you consistent service regardless of your final destination.
